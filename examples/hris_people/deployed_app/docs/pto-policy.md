# PTO & Time-Off Policy

**Category:** Time Off

This policy explains how paid time off (PTO) is earned and used at Acme Solutions.
It applies to all full-time employees. Part-time and contract staff accrue leave on
a pro-rated basis.

## Annual Leave

Full-time employees accrue **18 days** of annual leave per year, accrued monthly at
1.5 days per completed month of service. Employees with five or more years of tenure
accrue **22 days** per year. Up to 5 unused days may be carried over into the next
calendar year; carried-over days must be used by 31 March or they are forfeited.

Annual leave is requested through the People Portal and requires manager approval.
Requests of five or more consecutive days should be submitted at least two weeks in
advance.

## Sick Leave

Employees receive **14 days** of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate is
required for absences of three or more consecutive days. Unused sick leave does not
carry over and is not paid out.

## Wellbeing Days

Every employee receives **4 wellbeing days** per year, separate from annual and sick
leave. These can be taken at short notice, do not require a medical certificate, and
are intended for mental health, family matters, or rest.

## Parental Leave

- **Maternity:** 16 weeks fully paid, with the option of an additional 8 weeks at
  half pay.
- **Paternity / co-parent:** 4 weeks fully paid, to be taken within six months of
  the birth or adoption.
- A phased return-to-work schedule (reduced hours for the first month) is available
  on request.

## Public Holidays

Public holidays follow the calendar of each employee's home office (Bangkok,
Singapore, Ho Chi Minh City, or Jakarta). When a public holiday falls on a weekend, a
substitute day is granted in line with local regulations.
