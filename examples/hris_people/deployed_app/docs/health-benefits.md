# Health Insurance & Medical Benefits

**Category:** Benefits

Acme Solutions provides comprehensive health coverage to all full-time employees
from their first day of employment. Coverage extends to spouses and dependent
children. Contractors and managed-workforce staff are eligible for the Core plan
after 90 days of continuous engagement.

## Plan Tiers

We offer three medical plan tiers:

- **Core** — fully company-paid for the employee. Covers inpatient and outpatient
  care, an annual health check-up, and emergency treatment up to USD 50,000 per year.
- **Plus** — adds dental, optical (one pair of lenses or frames per year), and
  specialist consultations without referral. Employee contributes USD 25 per month.
- **Family** — extends Plus coverage to all registered dependents, raises the annual
  limit to USD 150,000, and includes maternity and newborn care. Employee contributes
  USD 80 per month.

## Mental Health & Wellbeing

All tiers include the Employee Assistance Programme (EAP): up to 12 confidential
counselling sessions per year at no cost, plus a 24/7 support line. Wellbeing days
(see the PTO policy) can be taken without a medical certificate.

## Enrolment & Claims

New joiners are auto-enrolled in the Core plan and may upgrade within 30 days of
joining, or during the annual open-enrolment window each November. Claims are
submitted through the People Portal; reimbursements are processed within 10 business
days. For cashless treatment, present your digital insurance card at any in-network
clinic across Thailand, Singapore, Vietnam, and Indonesia.

## Regional Notes

Coverage is underwritten locally in each country we operate in, so specific
in-network hospital lists differ by office. Singapore-based staff additionally
receive top-up coverage that integrates with MediSave. Contact People & HR for the
hospital panel that applies to your location.
