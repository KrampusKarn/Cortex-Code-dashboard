# Upcoming Company Events

**Category:** Events

A rolling calendar of company-wide events for 2026. All times are local to the
hosting office unless noted. RSVP links are posted in the People Portal and the
#events channel.

## July 2026

- **Q3 All-Hands (15 July, virtual + Bangkok HQ):** Quarterly business update from the
  leadership team, product roadmap preview, and live Q&A. Recording shared afterwards.
- **Engineering Guild Day (24 July, Singapore):** Internal tech talks, an
  architecture clinic, and a hands-on Cortex/AI workshop. Open to all regions via
  livestream.

## August 2026

- **Wellbeing Week (4–8 August, all offices):** Daily sessions on mental health,
  ergonomics, and financial wellbeing, plus subsidised health screenings. Use a
  wellbeing day to attend guilt-free.
- **Summer Social (22 August, Ho Chi Minh City):** Team dinner and awards night for
  the APAC delivery teams. Travel stipends available for cross-office attendees.

## September 2026

- **Hackathon "Build with Cortex" (11–12 September, hybrid):** 24-hour cross-functional
  hackathon. Winning teams present at the Q4 All-Hands. Sign-up closes 1 September.
- **New Joiner Bootcamp (29 September, Jakarta):** Onboarding intensive for employees
  who joined in Q3, covering tools, culture, and benefits.

## Recurring

- **Town Hall:** first Thursday of every month, 4:00 PM Bangkok time.
- **Lunch & Learn:** every other Wednesday, rotating topics proposed in #events.
- **Volunteering Day:** one paid day per quarter to support a local cause of your
  choice.
